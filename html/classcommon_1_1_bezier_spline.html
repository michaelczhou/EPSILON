template<int N_DEG, int N_DIM>
class common::BezierSpline< N_DEG, N_DIM >

Bezier spline class The the j-th segment evaluation is given by B_j(t) = s_j*\sum_{i=0}^{N_DEG} c_j^i * b_{N_DEG}^i(t-Tj/s_j) the s_j is the time scaling factor of the j-the segment
